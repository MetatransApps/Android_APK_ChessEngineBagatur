<p>This is the open source Bagatur Chess Engine with its own GUI (graphical user interface).</p>
<p>It is not as strong as Stockfish, Komodo, AlphaZero and Leela Chess Zero, but is strong enough to beat a grandmaster.</p>
<p>It has 16 strength levels. From level 5, it could shows the search information.</p>
<p>The ELO rating is about 3000 and the program has a unique playing style.</p>
<p>In general, the application demonstrates an application of artificial intelligence (AI) into the domain of chess.</p>
<p>To clarify, it is not an OEX chess engine and other chess engines cannot be imported into the GUI.</p>

<p>Playing instructions:</p>
<ol>
<li>Check the menu and make sure, the strength level is appropriate.</li>
<li>Select/deselect the Human/Computer buttons for both sides according to with which side you want to play and whether you play against computer or another person.</li>
<li>Use flip board button to change sides if you play with black.</li>
<li>Move piece by drag and drop or by selecting from/to squares.</li>
<li>If you want, you could use the move navigation buttons to go back and forward in the moves sequence.</li>
<li>It is recommended to check all settings in the menu and make sure you play with the option you like most (e.g. move animation speed, chess pieces set, colors).</li>
</ol>

<p>Features:</p>
<ol>
<li>FEN import/export/share.</li>
<li>Different modes: Human-Human, Human-Bagatur, Bagatur-Bagatur.</li>
<li>Different strengths levels.</li>
<li>The app could show the best moves line as well as the evaluation of the board position produced by Bagatur.</li>
<li>Navigation of played move's (first, previous, next, last).</li>
<li>Flipboard function.</li>
<li>Moving by drag and drop as well as by selecting from and to squares.</li>
<li>Supports different color palettes and chess pieces sets for different tastes.</li>
</ol>
